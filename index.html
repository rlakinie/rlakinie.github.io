About Me

Hello! My name is Robert Akinie and I am an aspiring Machine Learning Engineer/Data Scientist.

My interest in Machine Learning stemmed from my undergrad capstone project, employing computer vision and ladder logic programming in constructing a self-sorting trash can system. I have a knack for being excited in the face of complexity, and I enjoy puzzles and learning new technologies. Fast-forward to today, I am pursuing a doctorate degree in Electrical/Computer Engineering at NCA&T SU. with research interests in Clustering and Anomaly Detection in Time Series Data. Other focused areas include Classical Machine Learning, Neural Nets and Deep Learning.

Here are some of the few technologies I've been working with recently: C++, NumPy, Pandas, Matplotlib packages within Python.
